Modification steps:
1. Downloaded worldwide data in .tif aggregated form at a resolution of 1000m 
	- Data in Interrupted Goode Homolosine projection
2. Clipped raster to the approximate area of MN/Upper Midwest
3. Reprojected the layer into WGS84 (EPSG:4326, OGC:CRS84 - focus on upper midwest)
4. Clipped to the extent of the Land Used raster (-98, 43, -89, 50)
5. Saved as an R raster (.grd)

CNratio:

1. Calculated CN ratio as ((soc*10)/nitrogen) for both depths.
2. Wrote out as Geotifs and R grids
